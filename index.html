Dagona Coffee Recipe

By Chef Kavinisha

This yummy South Korean Coffee will make your mouth water. It only requires four ingredients that you can find in your home - you feel like you won the jackpot, right.

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Sugar
  • Milk

Dalgona Making Process

Step 1: Combine the Sugar, Hot Water, and Instant Coffee:
Add two tablespoons(tbsp) of sugar, 2 tbsp of coffee, two tbsp of hot water ina madium mixing bowl

Step 1: Mix until smooth and fluffy
Using an electric hand mixer, mix it until the coffee is fluffy and stiff peaked when touched

Step 3: Serve with Milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw, stir, stir, and stir to enjoy
